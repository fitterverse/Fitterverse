# Email Sequences

Three sequences for v1.

---

## Sequence A — Lead nurture (assessment-started, didn't pay)

Trigger: completed assessment without paying. 4-email flow over 5 days.

**Email 1 — 30 minutes after drop-off**
- Subject: Your 21-Day Reset plan is ready
- Body: Recap their assessment summary, show the personalized output, link back to payment.

**Email 2 — Day 2**
- Subject: One thing about Cohort [N] you should know
- Body: Show the cohort group concept + Champion prize structure. Soft re-pitch.

**Email 3 — Day 4**
- Subject: We close enrolment in 48 hours
- Body: Urgency. Limited seats. Re-link payment.

**Email 4 — Day 5 (last call)**
- Subject: Last 4 seats for Cohort [N]
- Body: Short. Just a link.

If they don't convert: move to "Future Cohort Waitlist" — 1 email per cohort opening.

---

## Sequence B — Cohort welcome (post-payment, pre-Day-1)

Trigger: payment confirmed.

**Email 1 — Immediate**
- Subject: You're in Cohort [N]. Here's what happens next.
- Body: Confirm payment, link assessment (if not done), what to expect, save WhatsApp number.

**Email 2 — T-3 (3 days before Day 1)**
- Subject: Three days till Day 1 — quick prep checklist
- Body: 5-item checklist (clear schedule, get a measuring tape, bookmark workout link, etc.)

**Email 3 — T-1 (day before Day 1)**
- Subject: Tomorrow we start.
- Body: Founder note. Excitement. WhatsApp group invite. "Day 1 photo by 11:59 PM tomorrow."

---

## Sequence C — Post-cohort (after Day 21)

Trigger: cohort completion.

**Email 1 — Day 22**
- Subject: 21 days done. Here's your full report.
- Body: Personalized summary (auto-generated), Champion submission link.

**Email 2 — Day 24 (Champion announcement)**
- Subject: Champions of Cohort [N] — and a thank you
- Body: Already in `03_Champion_Program/Winner_Announcement_Template.md`.

**Email 3 — Day 28**
- Subject: What's next for you (3 paths)
- Body: 
  - Option 1: Join Cohort [N+1] (₹200 graduate discount)
  - Option 2: Plus plan (₹1,499/month, ongoing accountability)
  - Option 3: Take a break (we'll send you a 1-page maintenance plan)

---

## Sender voice

All emails from "[Founder name], Fitterverse" — not from a brand name. Even at scale, founder-voice emails outperform brand emails 2-3x in this kind of program.

## Compliance

- Every email has unsubscribe link in footer
- Cohort completers are auto-enrolled in "Alumni" list (separate from prospect list)
- We do not buy email lists. Ever.
