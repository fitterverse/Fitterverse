## Production rules

- Vertical 9:16, 1080x1920
- ≤ 30 seconds (longer reels lose retention)
- First 2 seconds = hook (no intro, no logo)
- Subtitles burned in (most people watch on mute)
- One CTA per reel — never two
- Founder face in 60% of reels (build personal brand alongside Fitterverse brand)
- No before/after clickbait of strangers — only of consenting cohort members

## Caption template

```
Hook line (matching the visual hook).

Specific insight (one paragraph).

Why this matters for you (one paragraph).

CTA (one line):
- Comment RESET for the cohort
- Or: Save this for your next bad-food-day
- Or: Tag a friend who keeps restarting fitness
```
