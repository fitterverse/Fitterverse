# Ad Creative Briefs — Meta + Google

3 ad sets to launch with for Cohort 1.

---

## Ad Set 1 — "The Restarter" (Meta)

**Audience:** 25–40, metros (Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Mumbai, Pune, Delhi NCR), interests: fitness, weight loss, gym, yoga, healthy eating, Zomato, Swiggy.

**Objective:** Lead generation (form fill on landing page).

**Hook variants (test 3):**
- A: "Tired of restarting fitness every Monday?"
- B: "You don't need more motivation. You need a smaller plan."
- C: "Indian food. 20-min workouts. WhatsApp accountability. ₹999."

**Body:** "The 21-Day Fitterverse Reset is a WhatsApp-led cohort for busy Indian professionals. Plate method over calorie counting. Daily check-ins, real coach. Top 3 transformations win cash + free next cohort."

**CTA:** "Reserve my seat — ₹999"

**Visual:** Founder face + plate of Indian home food + phone showing WhatsApp coach reply.

**Budget:** ₹500/day, 7 days = ₹3,500

---

## Ad Set 2 — "The Champion" (Meta retargeting)

**Audience:** Anyone who visited fitterverse.in or engaged with our Instagram in last 30 days but didn't convert.

**Hook:**
- "Top 3 transformations in our cohort win ₹10K, ₹5K, ₹2.5K."

**Body:** "Every cohort, three Fitterverse Champions are crowned — judged on consistency, not crash dieting. ₹17,500 prize pool. Cohort starts [date]. 30 seats."

**CTA:** "Join Cohort [N]"

**Visual:** Mock Champion certificates + day-1/day-21 photos (post-cohort 1 we have real photos to use).

**Budget:** ₹250/day, 7 days = ₹1,750

---

## Ad Set 3 — Google Search

**Keyword groups:**
- "online fitness coach India" (high intent)
- "weight loss program India" (medium intent)
- "fitness accountability coach" (high intent)
- "diet plan for office workers" (medium intent)
- "21 day fitness challenge India" (high intent)
- "WhatsApp fitness coach" (high intent, low volume)

**Ad copy variant 1:**
> 21-Day Fitness Reset · ₹999
> WhatsApp coach. Indian food plan. 30 seats only. No app to install.
> Top 3 transformations win cash. Cohort starts [date].

**Ad copy variant 2:**
> Stop Restarting Fitness Every Monday
> Habit-based, WhatsApp-led, ₹999. Built for busy Indian professionals.
> Reserve a seat now.

**Landing page:** Always send to /reset (not homepage). Cohort-specific landing.

**Budget:** ₹400/day, 14 days = ₹5,600

---

## Total Cohort 1 ad spend

| Channel | Spend |
|---|---|
| Meta — cold audience | ₹3,500 |
| Meta — retargeting | ₹1,750 |
| Google Search | ₹5,600 |
| Total | **₹10,850** |

If we acquire 30 paid users at ₹999 with this spend, CAC = ₹362 and revenue = ₹29,970. Break-even on ads. The point of Cohort 1 is data, not profit.

---

## What we measure per ad set

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| CTR | ≥ 1.5% |
| Cost per landing page view | ≤ ₹15 |
| Landing page → assessment start | ≥ 25% |
| Assessment start → payment | ≥ 30% |
| Cost per paid user | ≤ ₹400 |

If any ad set blows ₹2,000 with no conversions, kill it. Iterate creative.
