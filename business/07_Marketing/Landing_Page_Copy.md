## Section 10 — FAQ

**How is this different from Cult.fit / Healthify / FITTR?**
> Cult.fit sells gym sessions. Healthify sells calorie tracking. FITTR sells ₹15K+ premium coaching. Fitterverse sells a 21-day habit reset on WhatsApp for ₹999, designed for people who don't want a gym, an app, or a strict diet.

**What if I miss a day?**
> Nothing happens. You don't lose access, you don't get shamed. Your coach DMs you. You start the next day. Consistency is the goal — perfection is not.

**Do I need a gym?**
> No. All workouts are bodyweight or minimal equipment. Optional gym version of every workout if you have access.

**Will I lose weight?**
> Most people do, but we don't promise numbers. What we promise is: if you do 80% of the program for 21 days, you will feel different — energy, sleep, food relationship, posture.

**What happens after 21 days?**
> Up to you. You can stop. You can join the next cohort. You can subscribe to the Plus plan (₹1,499/month) for ongoing accountability.

**What if I want a refund?**
> 100% refund up to Day 3. After Day 3, no refund (we've already invested in your onboarding and coach time). Honest exceptions handled by founder.
