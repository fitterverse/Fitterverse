## Locked-in decisions (v1)

| Decision | Choice |
|---|---|
| First niche | Busy urban professionals, age 25–40, metros |
| First product | 21-Day Reset cohort |
| Launch price | ₹999 |
| Delivery | WhatsApp + lightweight web dashboard |
| Champion prize | Top 3 transformations: cash + free next cohort (no merch in v1) |
| Operating model | Cohort-based, not subscription-first |

> "Fitterverse is the accountability partner for busy Indian professionals who want healthier eating and more consistent workouts without turning fitness into a second job."

## What success looks like for the first 3 cohorts

- Cohort 1 (Month 1): 30 paid users · 60% completion · 5 transformation case studies
- Cohort 2 (Month 2): 75 paid users · 65% completion · 12 case studies · ₹75K revenue
- Cohort 3 (Month 3): 150 paid users · 70% completion · 25 case studies · ₹1.5L revenue

After cohort 3 we evaluate: do we go subscription, scale cohorts, or open B2B corporate?

## The 4C Method (our brand IP)

1. **Clarity** — know your goal, baseline, routine, blockers
2. **Consistency** — small daily habits before aggressive transformation
3. **Context** — Indian food, festivals, office life, family
4. **Coaching** — AI + human accountability keeps you moving

Every program, piece of content, and feature should ladder back to one of these four.
