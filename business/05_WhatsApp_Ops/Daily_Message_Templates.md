# WhatsApp Daily Message Templates

Every cohort day has 4 scheduled messages. Coach personalizes the morning and evening; midday + wind-down are templated/automated.

Tone rules across all messages:
- No exclamation marks (≤ 1 per message)
- No "you should" / "you must"
- Short — never more than 3 lines unless it's the morning kick-off
- One emoji max
- Always end with one micro-action
