# Coach Standard Operating Procedure

The coach is the product. Everything else is delivery infrastructure.

---

## Coach load (capacity rules)

- 1 coach manages **up to 100 users** per cohort
- Hard cap: 1 coach handling more than 100 = quality drops, retention falls
- Trigger to hire coach #2: enrolment crosses 80 in any cohort

## Daily routine (4 hrs/day during cohort)

| Time | Activity | Duration |
|---|---|---|
| 9:00–9:30 AM | Review overnight check-ins; reply to unanswered DMs | 30 min |
| 10:00–11:00 AM | Personal morning DMs (5 priority users — most at-risk) | 60 min |
| 1:00–2:00 PM | Lunch plate review (review 10 random plate photos) | 60 min |
| 3:00–3:30 PM | Mid-day check-in batch | 30 min |
| 7:00–8:00 PM | Evening 1:1 DMs (5 users — different from morning batch) | 60 min |
| 9:30–10:00 PM | End-of-day batch reply | 30 min |

Total: 4 hours/day. Sundays add 1.5 hours for weekly reports.

## Weekly cadence

| Day | Special action |
|---|---|
| Day 1 | Personal welcome message to every user (~30 sec each, ~50 min total) |
| Day 4 | Plate Audit feedback — coach reviews 1 plate per user |
| Day 7 | Week-1 report send + 1:1 reflection prompts |
| Day 11 | Mid-cohort 1:1 — coach DMs every user a personal review (highest leverage day) |
| Day 14 | Week-2 report send + progress photo reminder |
| Day 18 | Champion submission preview — coach mentions to top performers |
| Day 21 | Final progress prompts + Champion submission link |
| Day 22 | Submission review (verify completeness) |
| Day 23 | Champion judging |

## Priority triage — who gets coach attention

Every morning, the dashboard shows users ranked by need:

1. **Red — at risk** (3+ missed days in a row, or low energy reports, or "wants to quit" messages)
2. **Yellow — slipping** (2 missed days, or habit score dropping for 3 days)
3. **Green — on track**
4. **Gold — exceeding** (hitting all targets, candidate for Champion mention)

Coach time allocation rule: 50% Red + 30% Yellow + 10% Gold + 10% Green.

Greens get the standard automated nudges. They don't need extra love unless they ask.

## Voice and tone (every coach must learn this)

| Don't | Do |
|---|---|
| "You should" | "What if you tried" |
| "You missed" | "Didn't see you yesterday" |
| "Let's push harder" | "Let's reset and keep going" |
| "Strict diet" | "Plate method" |
| "Workout discipline" | "Showing up" |
| "Cheat day" | "Mindful indulgence" |
| "Calories" | We don't say this in v1 |
| "Cleanse / detox" | We don't say this ever |

Always end with one specific micro-action.

---

## What coaches can decide

Coaches have authority to:
- Adjust daily plan for a specific user (lighter workout, alternate food)
- Approve a "pause day" (no workout, just check-in)
- Issue ₹200 referral discount codes
- Recommend a user to Champion finalist list (founder confirms)

## What coaches cannot decide

Coaches escalate to founder/medical for:
- Medical issues (any new diagnosis, medication, pregnancy, injury)
- Refund requests
- Disqualification calls
- Anti-cheat flags
- Anything outside the playbook (creative requests, personal training, etc.)

## Tools the coach uses daily

1. **Coach dashboard** (web) — sees all assigned users, tracker data, alerts, message history
2. **WhatsApp Business app** — for direct messaging
3. **Notion (or Airtable)** — for SOP, templates, escalation log
4. **Razorpay coach view** — to verify enrolment status
5. **Calendly** — for "wants to quit" rescue calls

---

## Coach onboarding (before they touch a user)

A new coach goes through this in week 1 of joining:
- Day 1–2: Read all docs in `02_Cohort_Program/`, `04_Onboarding/`, `05_WhatsApp_Ops/`, `06_Coach_Ops/`
- Day 3: Shadow founder/lead coach replying to a live cohort
- Day 4: Mock-handle 5 user scenarios (including "wants to quit", "medical", "asking about supplements")
- Day 5: Take 5 real users from a current cohort
- Day 6–10: Get feedback from lead coach on every message sent
- Day 11+: Take a full 50-user batch in next cohort

## Coach metrics (how we evaluate coaches)

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Response time on user DM | < 4 hours during work hours |
| Daily 1:1 DMs sent | ≥ 10 |
| Day-11 personal review sent to all assigned users | 100% |
| Cohort completion rate of their users | ≥ 60% |
| Cohort NPS of their users | ≥ 40 |
| User-reported "felt heard by coach" | ≥ 80% |

Bonuses for coaches: ₹200 per Champion they coached, ₹2,000 if their cohort completion rate > 70%.
