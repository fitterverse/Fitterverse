# Coach's Day — Hour-by-Hour Routine

This is what a coach actually does, every day during a cohort.

---

## 8:30 AM — Wake-up review (15 min)

- Open coach dashboard
- Scan overnight messages (anyone reaching out late at night?)
- Note any "Red" flagged users for the day's priority list

## 9:00 AM — Morning batch reply (30 min)

- Reply to every overnight message (target: cleared queue by 9:30)
- Personal tone, never templated

## 9:30 AM — Auto-message verification (5 min)

- Confirm 7:00 AM auto-message went out (BSP dashboard)
- Spot-check 3 random user inboxes

## 10:00 AM — Morning 1:1s (60 min)

- Send 5 personalized DMs to priority Red users
- Each DM references their specific tracker pattern
- Goal: 1 reply minimum from each by noon

## 11:00 AM — Plate review prep (free time / content / admin)

- Coach is off-rotation; admin work, content creation, training

## 1:00 PM — Lunch plate review (60 min)

- Open plate-photo queue
- Review 10–15 plates randomly across users
- Reply with one specific suggestion per plate
- Pull 2 best plates for cohort group share (with permission)

## 2:00 PM — Lunch break

## 3:00 PM — Mid-day check (30 min)

- Reply to incoming DMs
- Check who hasn't done their workout yet (auto-flagged)
- Send mid-afternoon nudge to 5–10 unstarted users

## 3:30–6:30 PM — Free / admin / content

## 7:00 PM — Evening 1:1s (60 min)

- 5 different personalized DMs (different users from morning)
- Includes Yellow users from triage
- Voice notes for Red users (if needed)

## 8:00 PM — Cohort group moderation (15 min)

- Reply to questions in cohort WhatsApp group
- Pin one positive moment from the day
- Quietly DM anyone posting concerning content (extreme dieting talk, body comparison)

## 9:30 PM — End-of-day batch (30 min)

- Final DM sweep
- Confirm 9:30 PM auto-message went
- Tag any user who needs follow-up tomorrow morning (Red list seeding)

## 10:00 PM — Off duty

---

## Sunday additional (90 min)

- Generate weekly report drafts in dashboard (auto)
- Add personalized 2-line note to each user's report
- Trigger 7:00 PM Sunday send

| Cohort day | Total coach hours |
|---|---|
| Day 1 | 5 hrs (welcome message to every user) |
| Day 4 | 5 hrs (plate audit) |
| Day 11 | 6 hrs (1:1 review for every user) |
| Day 21 | 5 hrs (final submission support) |
| Day 23 | 4 hrs (Champion judging participation) |

---

## What the coach should NEVER do

- Take user calls outside of pre-scheduled "rescue calls"
- Give medical advice
- Recommend supplements, fat burners, or specific brands
- Become emotionally entangled with a user (referral to founder if a user gets dependent)
- Promise specific weight loss numbers
- Move conversations off WhatsApp (no Instagram DM, no personal phone)
