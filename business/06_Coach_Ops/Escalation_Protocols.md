# Coach Escalation Protocols

When the coach hits something outside their authority, this doc says what to do.

---

## Tier 1 — Coach handles independently

- General nutrition guidance (within plate-method framework)
- Workout modifications (knee, back, time-constrained)
- Motivation, encouragement, group moderation
- Lifestyle adjustments (sleep, water, steps, eating out)
- Cohort group conflicts (warning, removal if needed)

## Tier 2 — Escalate to lead coach within 4 hours

- New injury during cohort
- Repeated late-night anxiety messages
- User asking detailed nutrition for medical condition (PCOS, thyroid, diabetes)
- User expressing concerning eating patterns (skipping meals deliberately, extreme restriction)
- Cohort group dispute coach can't resolve

**Action:** Tag lead coach in dashboard with "ESC2" and one-line context.

## Tier 3 — Escalate to founder within 1 hour

- Refund request
- Anti-cheat flag (suspected fabrication)
- User mentions suicidal thoughts, severe mental distress
- Pregnancy disclosure mid-cohort
- Major medical event (chest pain, fainting, hospitalization)
- Press / media / legal contact

**Action:** Direct WhatsApp to founder with full context. Founder responds within 1 hour.

## Tier 4 — Immediate emergency

- Active medical emergency described in messages
- Anything criminal or legal (harassment, threats)

**Action:**
1. If life-threatening medical: tell user to call emergency services immediately (108 in India). Do not delay with messaging.
2. Notify founder by phone call (not text).
3. Document everything in writing within 24 hours.

## Specific scripts

### User mentions suicidal thoughts or severe mental distress

Coach reply (within 30 min):

> [First name], I hear you. I'm not the right person to help with this — and I want to make sure you get the right support.
>
> Please reach out to one of these now:
> - iCall: 9152987821 (Mon–Sat, 8 AM–10 PM)
> - Vandrevala Foundation: 1860-2662-345 (24/7)
>
> I'll check in with you again in 6 hours. You're not alone in this.

Then: notify founder immediately. Pause cohort participation for that user (no daily reminders). Founder follows up in 24 hours.

### User reveals eating disorder history

Coach reply:

> Thank you for telling me. I want to be straight with you — Fitterverse is a habit-and-fitness program, not a clinical eating disorder support program.
>
> I'd like to offer a full refund and pause your participation in this cohort. We can discuss a lighter, non-weight-focused version of the program, but only with sign-off from a qualified therapist or doctor.
>
> What feels right to you?

Then: escalate Tier 3.

### User reveals pregnancy mid-cohort

Coach reply:

> Congratulations. We're going to pause your participation here — Fitterverse v1 isn't designed for prenatal fitness, and I don't want to give you advice that's outside my training.
>
> We'll refund the unused portion. When you're ready postpartum, we have a separate program in development — I'll keep you on the waitlist.

Then: escalate Tier 3 + add to postpartum waitlist.

### User asks for refund

Day 1–3: Full refund, no questions.
Day 4–7: 50% refund.
Day 8+: No refund (per published policy), but we honor compelling cases at founder's discretion.

Coach script:
> I hear you. Per our refund policy, [X]. I've forwarded this to [founder name] and they'll follow up within 24 hours.

Then: escalate Tier 3 with one-line user context.

### User asks for medical specifics (e.g. PCOS diet plan)

Coach reply:
> That's a doctor-and-dietitian question, and I'm trained to do habit coaching, not medical nutrition therapy.
>
> What I can do: keep your habit floor steady (water, walks, protein, sleep, plate method). Whatever your doctor or dietitian says, I'll work around it.
>
> Want me to introduce you to a registered dietitian who works with us? (Available in Plus plan post-cohort.)
