# Fitterverse Champion Program

> "Every cohort produces 3 Champions. They are the proof that this works."

The Champion Program is the single most important marketing asset Fitterverse owns. Every cohort generates 3 named, documented transformations that compound into a transformation library no competitor can copy.

---

## What it is

At the end of every 21-day cohort, the top 3 transformations are crowned **Fitterverse Champions** — Gold, Silver, Bronze.

It is **not** a "biggest weight loss" contest. It rewards transformation across four dimensions, with consistency weighted highest. This is intentional: we don't want to reward extreme behavior, we want to reward exactly what we sell — sustainable habit change.

---

## Eligibility

Anyone enrolled in a cohort is eligible if they:
1. Completed at least 18 of 21 daily check-ins
2. Submitted Day-1 and Day-21 weight, waist, and progress photo
3. Submitted a written reflection (the Champion Submission, see template)
4. Did not violate the cohort group rules
5. Opted in to public sharing of their story

Users who don't want their story public are still eligible for **silent recognition** (badge in app, no public post).

## Prize structure (locked-in v1)

| Rank | Cash prize | Free continuation | Recognition |
|---|---|---|---|
| Gold (1st) | ₹10,000 | Free seat in next cohort | Featured case study + Hall of Fame |
| Silver (2nd) | ₹5,000 | Free seat in next cohort | Featured case study + Hall of Fame |

Total prize pool per cohort: **₹17,500 cash + 3 free seats (≈ ₹3,000 retail value)**

No merch in v1. We add Fitterverse-branded items in v2 once the brand has weight.

---

## Bonus recognition (every cohort, no limit)

Beyond Top 3, we recognize three more categories. These get a public shoutout but no cash:

- **Most Consistent** — highest daily check-in score (zero missed)
- **Biggest Comeback** — biggest improvement after a tough week 1
- **Group MVP** — most encouraging cohort member (peer-voted)

This means up to 6 people get publicly celebrated each cohort. More celebration = more UGC = more inbound for next cohort.

---

## Why this works (strategic notes)

1. **Cash prize ≈ what one user paid.** ₹10K is meaningful but not break-the-budget — it's roughly the LTV of one strong customer.
2. **Free next cohort + champions stay in the community.** This means our most successful users keep coming back as alumni — they become unpaid peer coaches and content creators.
3. **3 winners + 3 bonus categories = 6 stories per cohort.** Run 12 cohorts in a year and you have 70+ documented transformations. Few Indian fitness brands can match that volume of named proof.
4. **Public stories drive next cohort enrolment.** We launch the next cohort the same week we announce winners — the FOMO cycle compounds.
5. **Judging on consistency (not pure weight loss) protects the brand.** No one ever gets a Champion title for crash dieting.

## Anti-gaming rules

- Day-1 photo must be timestamped and submitted within 24 hours of cohort start
- Day-21 photo must be in same lighting, similar clothing, similar pose to Day 1
- Weight and waist measurements: video-confirm in 1 random user per Top 5 (zoom call)
- Anyone caught fabricating measurements or photos is disqualified and removed from the cohort with a 50% refund
- Coaches can flag suspicious submissions; flagged submissions go to founder for final call

---

## How winners are decided

See `Judging_Rubric.md` for the full scoring system. Summary:

- 40% Consistency (check-in days, habit score)
- 30% Physical change (weight, waist, progress photo blind-judge)
- 15% Habit transformation (sleep, water, protein, steps)
- 15% Story quality (reflection writing, depth of insight)

Three judges:
1. Lead coach
2. Founder
3. One previous Champion (from cohort 2 onwards)
