# Champion Submission Template

This is what every cohort user fills out on Day 22 to be considered for Champion.

---

## Submission form fields

### Section 1: Identity & permission

- Full name
- City
- Age
- Phone (for prize transfer)
- UPI ID (for cash prize)
- **I consent to my story, photos, and quotes being used publicly by Fitterverse for marketing purposes.** (Yes / No / Yes-but-anonymous)

If "No" or "Yes-but-anonymous", user is eligible for prize but story is published with face/name redacted or replaced.

### Section 3: Photos

- Day 1 photo: front, side, back (already uploaded)
- Day 21 photo: front, side, back (upload now)
- Same lighting, similar clothing, similar pose

### Section 4: The Reflection (the part judges score on)

Answer each question in 3–6 sentences. Be specific. Be honest. The best entries are particular, not generic.

**Q1. Where were you, fitness-wise, on the morning of Day 1?**
*(Be specific. What was your routine? What was your relationship with food, your body, your energy?)*

**Q2. What was the hardest day, and what did you do?**
*(We are looking for the day you almost quit and what kept you going.)*

**Q3. What is one specific behavior that has changed?**
*(Don't say "I eat better." Say what specifically you do differently — at what time, in what context.)*

**Q4. What is your honest plan for the next 21 days, on your own?**
*(We are looking for sustainability, not promises. "I will keep walking 8000 steps and one protein meal at lunch" beats "I will work out every day for life.")*

**Q5. What would you tell someone signing up for the next cohort?**
*(One sentence. This becomes a quote we may use publicly.)*

### Section 5: Optional

- Anything else you want the judges to know
- Anyone in the cohort who deserves recognition (peer nomination for "Group MVP")
