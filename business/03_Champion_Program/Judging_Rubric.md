# Champion Judging Rubric

Total score = 100 points. Top 3 by total score win.

---

## Dimension 1: Consistency (40 points)

The single biggest weight. We reward showing up, not extreme effort.

| Sub-metric | Points | How |
|---|---|---|
| Daily check-in count (out of 21) | 15 | (count / 21) × 15 |
| Workouts completed (out of 14 prescribed) | 10 | (count / 14) × 10 |
| Plate method days logged (out of 21) | 8 | (count / 21) × 8 |
| Sleep target hit (out of 21) | 7 | (count / 21) × 7 |

**Floor:** Anyone with < 18 check-ins is automatically ineligible.

---

## Dimension 2: Physical change (30 points)

Judged blind by 3 judges who see only Day-1 vs Day-21 photo, weight delta %, waist delta %.

| Sub-metric | Points | Method |
|---|---|---|
| Photo transformation (visible posture, definition, presence) | 12 | 3 judges score 0–4 each, average × 3 |
| Weight delta (% of starting) | 9 | 0.5% loss = 1pt, 1% = 3pt, 2% = 6pt, 3%+ = 9pt |
| Waist delta (cm) | 9 | 1 cm = 2pt, 2 cm = 4pt, 3 cm = 6pt, 5cm+ = 9pt |

**Important:** This dimension is capped at 30 points. Someone with 6 kg loss does not get more than someone with 3 kg loss + better consistency. We are not paying out for crash dieting.

---

## Dimension 3: Habit transformation (15 points)

Compared between Week 1 average and Week 3 average from their tracker.

| Sub-metric | Points | How |
|---|---|---|
| Steps improvement (avg/day W3 - W1) | 4 | +1k = 1pt, +2k = 2pt, +3k = 3pt, +4k+ = 4pt |
| Water improvement | 3 | +0.5L = 1pt, +1L = 2pt, +1.5L+ = 3pt |
| Protein meals/day improvement | 4 | +0.5 = 1pt, +1 = 2pt, +1.5+ = 4pt |
| Sleep regularity improvement | 4 | std-dev of bedtime improved by 30 min = 2pt, by 60 min = 4pt |

---

## Dimension 4: Story quality (15 points)

Judges read all submission reflections blind (names redacted).

| Criterion | Points |
|---|---|
| Specific, honest, particular (not generic) | 5 |
| Clear before-state and clear after-state | 4 |
| One concrete behavior change they can articulate | 3 |
| Sustainability mindset — not "I'm cured" but "I'm continuing" | 3 |

---

## Tie-breaker order

1. Higher Consistency score wins
2. Then higher Habit transformation score
3. Then earlier Day-1 photo submission timestamp
4. Then coach's qualitative judgment

---

## Judges' protocol

- All scoring is independent; judges do not see each other's scores
- All identifying info (name, photo before scoring) is hidden in dimensions 3 and 4
- Photos in dimension 2 are shown blind (no name, no caption)
- Combined score = sum of three judges, divided by 3
- Founder has final call only on disqualification (anti-cheat), not on rank

---

## Worked example

| User | Consistency (40) | Physical (30) | Habit (15) | Story (15) | **Total** |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| User A | 38 | 22 | 13 | 12 | **85** ← Gold |
| User B | 35 | 28 | 11 | 10 | **84** ← Silver |
| User C | 36 | 18 | 12 | 14 | **80** ← Bronze |
| User D | 30 | 26 | 9 | 9 | 74 |
| User E | 21 | 12 | 8 | 10 | 51 (eligible but low) |
| User F | 17 | — | — | — | INELIGIBLE (< 18 check-ins) |

Notice User B had the biggest physical change but lost Gold to User A who was more consistent. That is the right outcome for what Fitterverse stands for.
