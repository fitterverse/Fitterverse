# Winner Announcement Templates

Use these templates verbatim (with name/numbers swapped) for every cohort.

---

## 1. Instagram Live (Day 24, 7:30 PM)

15-minute show. Format:
- 0–2 min: Founder welcome + cohort recap (size, completion rate, one big stat)
- 2–4 min: Bronze winner — name, city, one photo, 30-sec call-in
- 4–6 min: Silver winner — same format
- 6–10 min: Gold winner — name, city, one photo, full 3-min interview
- 10–13 min: 3 bonus categories (Most Consistent, Biggest Comeback, Group MVP)
- 13–15 min: Next cohort enrolment opens — link in bio, 30 seats

## 4. Long-form blog post per Champion (Day 25–26)

Title format: "How [Name] [specific change] in 21 days — Cohort [N] Gold Champion"

Length: 800–1200 words. Sections:
- The Day-1 reality (use Q1 from their submission)
- The hardest moment (Q2)
- What changed, specifically (Q3)
- Their forward plan (Q4)
- Their advice to the next person (Q5)
- Photos: Day 1, mid-cohort, Day 21
- Sidebar: their habit score numbers, sleep delta, protein delta
- CTA at end: "Cohort [N+1] starts [date]"

This is an SEO asset. Each Champion blog post should target one long-tail search:
- "21 day fitness transformation Indian"
- "how to lose weight in 3 weeks without diet"
- "fitness for software engineers India"
- etc. — choose based on the Champion's profile
