# Vision and Mission

## Vision
A fitter, calmer, more energetic India — built through daily accountability for food, movement, and recovery.

## Mission
Help one million busy Indian professionals build health routines that survive real life — through diet accountability, workout consistency, Indian-context guidance, and human + AI support.

## Core beliefs
- People don't fail because they don't know what to do. They fail because their plan can't survive a Tuesday meeting, a Friday dinner, or a Diwali.
- Tracking is not transformation. Daily execution plus accountability is.
- Indian food is not a problem to be solved with quinoa. It's the medium our customers actually live in.
- Guilt breaks the habit loop. Empathy rebuilds it.
- Cohorts beat apps for early-stage behavior change because identity ("I'm doing the Reset") is stickier than features.

## What we will not do
- Promise fast weight loss numbers ("lose 10 kg in 30 days")
- Sell crash diets, detox teas, fat burners, supplements as the lead product
- Make medical claims (diabetes cure, PCOS cure, thyroid reversal)
- Body-shame, fear-monger, or use "no excuses" tone
- Build a generic tracker that forgets execution and accountability

## Brand voice
- Calm, not hype
- Empathetic, not strict
- Practical, not perfectionist
- Indian-context, not borrowed Western
- Confident in science, humble about individual variation

## One-line positioning
> The accountability partner that turns "I'll start Monday" into consistent diet and workout follow-through.
