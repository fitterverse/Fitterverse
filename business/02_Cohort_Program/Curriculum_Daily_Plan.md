# 21-Day Curriculum — Day-by-Day

Every day has: theme · workout · nutrition focus · habit · evening reflection prompt.

---

## Week 1 — Foundation: "Show up, even imperfectly."

**Day 1 — Reset Day**
- Theme: Just begin
- Workout: 15-min beginner full-body (bodyweight)
- Nutrition: Add protein to one meal (eggs / paneer / dal / curd)
- Habit: Drink 2.5L water
- Reflection: What's one thing you fear about these 21 days?

**Day 2 — Walk it Out**
- Theme: Movement counts even when you don't feel like it
- Workout: 30-min walk (any time, any pace)
- Nutrition: Cut sugary chai/coffee to 2 max
- Habit: Track your steps
- Reflection: Where did you walk today?

**Day 3 — Strength Starter**
- Theme: Resistance is your friend
- Workout: 20-min bodyweight strength (squats, push-ups, planks)
- Nutrition: Eat protein at breakfast (no exceptions today)
- Habit: 3 meals at roughly the same time
- Reflection: How did your body feel after strength?

**Day 4 — Plate Audit**
- Theme: Look at your plate honestly
- Workout: 30-min walk
- Nutrition: Photo every plate today; coach reviews 1 per user
- Habit: Half plate veg at dinner
- Reflection: What did you eat that surprised you?

**Day 5 — Sleep Foundation**
- Theme: Sleep is fitness too
- Workout: 15-min mobility / yoga
- Nutrition: No food after 9 PM
- Habit: Phone out of bedroom
- Reflection: What time did you actually sleep?

**Day 6 — Strength Round 2**
- Theme: Repeat builds skill
- Workout: 20-min bodyweight strength (same as Day 3)
- Nutrition: Protein at 2 meals
- Habit: 7,000 steps
- Reflection: Easier or harder than Day 3?

**Day 7 — Reflection + Reset**
- Theme: One week down
- Workout: 30-min slow walk + 10 min stretch
- Nutrition: One mindful indulgence; eat slowly, no guilt
- Habit: Weekly weigh-in + waist measurement
- Reflection: What's the one habit that stuck this week?

---

## Week 2 — Build: "Add structure to consistency."

**Day 8 — The Indian Plate**
- Theme: Eat what you've always eaten, just better
- Workout: 25-min full-body strength
- Nutrition: Plate method (½ veg, ¼ protein, ¼ carb) at lunch + dinner
- Habit: 7,500 steps
- Reflection: Was the plate easier or harder than tracking calories?

**Day 9 — Cardio Day**
- Theme: Get the heart up
- Workout: 25-min brisk walk or jog intervals
- Nutrition: Carb-light dinner (skip rice/roti, more sabzi + protein)
- Habit: 3L water
- Reflection: How was the carb-light dinner?

**Day 10 — Protein Push**
- Theme: Protein is the lever
- Workout: 20-min upper body
- Nutrition: Hit protein target (see your assessment output) — coach reviews everyone
- Habit: Track protein grams
- Reflection: What protein source surprised you?

**Day 11 — Mid-Cohort Check**
- Theme: Where are you, really?
- Workout: Rest or 30-min walk
- Nutrition: No restrictions, just plate method
- Habit: Coach 1:1 review (every user gets a personal note)
- Reflection: What is one thing you're proud of so far?

**Day 12 — Strength + Steps**
- Theme: Compound the wins
- Workout: 25-min strength + 20-min walk
- Nutrition: 3 protein meals
- Habit: 8,000 steps
- Reflection: Easiest part of today?

**Day 13 — Eating Out / Travel Practice**
- Theme: Real life happens
- Workout: 20-min mobility
- Nutrition: Eat one meal outside home / at office cafeteria — apply plate method
- Habit: Hydrate before the meal
- Reflection: What did you order? Why?

**Day 14 — Halfway Win**
- Theme: 14 days of you showing up
- Workout: Pick your favorite workout from the past 13 days
- Nutrition: Mindful indulgence — your choice, no guilt
- Habit: Weigh-in + waist + progress photo
- Reflection: What is week 3 going to look like?

---

## Week 3 — Lock-in: "Make it identity, not effort."

**Day 15 — Identity Day**
- Theme: I am someone who...
- Workout: 30-min strength (push-pull-squat circuit)
- Nutrition: Build your "default day" meal plan
- Habit: 8,500 steps
- Reflection: Complete the sentence: "I am someone who ___"

**Day 16 — Recovery Matters**
- Theme: You can't grow without recovery
- Workout: 20-min yoga / mobility
- Nutrition: Add fiber (oats, fruit, salad)
- Habit: 8 hours in bed
- Reflection: How do you feel after a recovery day?

**Day 17 — Strength Peak**
- Theme: Push the floor up
- Workout: 30-min full-body strength (more reps than Day 6)
- Nutrition: Protein at every meal
- Habit: 9,000 steps
- Reflection: What got stronger?

**Day 18 — Tell Us Your Win**
- Theme: Capture it
- Workout: 30-min cardio (walk/jog)
- Nutrition: Plate method
- Habit: Write a 3-sentence win story (becomes testimonial seed)
- Reflection: Why did this win matter?

**Day 19 — The Off-Day Test**
- Theme: How do you respond to an off-day?
- Workout: Optional — your choice
- Nutrition: Whatever life throws at you; apply plate method where possible
- Habit: Don't break the streak; check in even if you ate poorly
- Reflection: What did you do when things didn't go to plan?

**Day 20 — Plan Week 4**
- Theme: What's your maintenance plan?
- Workout: 30-min favorite workout
- Nutrition: Default day meal plan, executed
- Habit: Write your post-cohort plan (template provided)
- Reflection: What's the smallest version of this you can do forever?

**Day 21 — Finale**
- Theme: You did it
- Workout: 45-min — your favorite, longer
- Nutrition: Celebration meal (mindful, planned)
- Habit: Final weigh-in + waist + progress photo + Champion submission
- Reflection: What changed in 21 days?

---

## Notes on programming

- All workouts are bodyweight or minimal equipment (resistance band optional)
- Two video versions: home (no equipment) and gym (basic equipment)
- Modifications provided for: knee issues, lower-back issues, beginner-extreme
- All workouts ≤ 30 minutes; users with < 15 min do the express version
- No weight-loss number promises in any communication
