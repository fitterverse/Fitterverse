# Nutrition Framework — The Fitterverse Plate Method

We do not count calories in v1. We use the Plate Method because it works for Indian home food, restaurants, and office cafeterias without an app.

---

## The Plate (the only image users need to remember)

```
+-----------------------------+
|                             |
|        VEG / SABZI          |
|         (½ plate)           |
|                             |
+--------------+--------------+
|   PROTEIN    |    CARB      |
|   (¼ plate)  |   (¼ plate)  |
+--------------+--------------+
```

- **Half plate veg/sabzi:** any cooked veg, salad, sambar with veg, dal+veg combos
- **Quarter plate protein:** dal, paneer, eggs, chicken, fish, curd, soya, sprouts, rajma, chana
- **Quarter plate carb:** rice, roti, dosa, idli, oats, poha, biryani base

## Daily protein target (rule of thumb)

`Target grams = bodyweight (kg) × 1.2`
(We round to nearest 10g for simplicity)

Examples:
- 60 kg → 70 g/day
- 75 kg → 90 g/day
- 90 kg → 110 g/day

## What we tell users (the 5 simple rules)

1. **Plate method at lunch and dinner.** Breakfast can be flexible.
2. **Protein at every meal.** Even 1 egg or ½ cup dal counts.
3. **2.5–3L water.** Carry a 1L bottle.
4. **One mindful indulgence per day.** Pre-decided. No guilt.
5. **No food after 9 PM** (or 2.5 hours before sleep).

## Things we do NOT do

- We do not prescribe calorie counts in v1
- We do not eliminate any food group (no "no rice", "no roti")
- We do not recommend supplements as a default
- We do not endorse keto, intermittent fasting, or any specific diet
- We do not give medical nutrition therapy (diabetes, PCOS, thyroid get general guidance + a "consult your doctor" disclaimer)

## Special notes

- **Vegetarians:** We make sure the plate has at least 2 protein sources (e.g. dal + paneer)
- **Vegan:** Soya, tofu, lentils, chickpeas, peanuts; supplement B12 (recommend they consult a doctor)
- **Eggitarian:** Eggs are the easiest win; aim for 2-3/day
- **Non-veg:** Chicken/fish/eggs over red meat; portion = palm size

## Cohort coach script for Day 4 (Plate Audit)

> "Today, photograph every plate before you eat. Don't change anything. We just want to see your real day. Tomorrow we'll show you exactly two changes that will move the needle."

This sets up Day 5 personal feedback and is the single highest-engagement moment of week 1.
