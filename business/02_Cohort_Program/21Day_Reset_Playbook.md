# 21-Day Fitterverse Reset — Master Playbook

This is the operating manual for running one cohort end-to-end. Anyone running a cohort should be able to execute it from this doc.

---

## Cohort lifecycle (the 7 phases)

| Phase | Days | Owner |
|---|---|---|
| 1. Pre-launch | T-21 to T-7 | Marketing |
| 2. Enrolment | T-7 to T-1 | Marketing + Ops |
| 3. Onboarding | T-2 to T-0 | Coach + Ops |
| 4. Reset days 1–7 (Foundation) | Day 1–7 | Coach |
| 5. Reset days 8–14 (Build) | Day 8–14 | Coach |
| 6. Reset days 15–21 (Lock-in) | Day 15–21 | Coach |
| 7. Wrap + Champion announcement | Day 22–25 | Coach + Marketing |

---

## Phase 1 — Pre-launch (T-21 to T-7)

- Lock cohort start date (always a Monday)
- Set seat cap (Cohort 1 = 30, Cohort 2 = 75, Cohort 3 = 150)
- Publish cohort landing page with countdown
- Begin daily reels, lead-magnet ad campaigns
- Open early-bird waitlist (₹100 hold; converts to full ₹999 by T-7)

## Phase 2 — Enrolment (T-7 to T-1)

- Enrolment opens — payment ₹999 unlocks the assessment form
- After payment: user gets WhatsApp welcome + assessment link
- All assessments must be completed by T-2
- Last 48 hours: send 3 reminders to incomplete assessments
- T-1: send "see you tomorrow" message + cohort group invite (if using a group)

## Phase 3 — Onboarding (T-2 to T-0)

- Each user receives:
  - Personalized output (BMI, calorie range, protein target, step goal — see `04_Onboarding/`)
  - Day 1 plan
  - Habit tracker access
  - Coach intro message
- T-0 (Sunday before Day 1): Founder/coach goes live on Instagram for 15 min — energy + welcome

## Phase 4 — Days 1–7: Foundation

**Theme: "Show up, even imperfectly."**

| Habit | Daily target |
|---|---|
| Move | 20-min beginner workout, 3x in week 1 |
| Steps | 6,000/day baseline |
| Eat | One protein-anchored meal per day |
| Hydrate | 2.5L water |
| Sleep | Lights out by 11:30 PM, 4 nights |
| Check-in | Daily WhatsApp check-in: 1 emoji + 1 line |

Coach actions:
- Day 1: motivational kickoff message + "what's your one fear about this cohort?"
- Day 3: first nudge for missed users
- Day 7: end-of-week reflection prompt + cohort leaderboard preview

## Phase 5 — Days 8–14: Build

**Theme: "Add structure to consistency."**

| Habit | Daily target |
|---|---|
| Move | 25-min workout, 4x in week |
| Steps | 7,500/day |
| Eat | Two protein-anchored meals; cap added sugar; carb-light dinner |
| Hydrate | 3L water |
| Sleep | 11 PM lights out, 5 nights |
| Check-in | Daily |

Coach actions:
- Day 8: introduce "Indian plate method" (half plate veg, quarter protein, quarter carbs)
- Day 11: mid-cohort 1:1 nudge — coach DMs every user a personal review
- Day 14: progress photo + weight + waist measurement

## Phase 6 — Days 15–21: Lock-in

**Theme: "Make it identity, not effort."**

| Habit | Daily target |
|---|---|
| Move | 30-min workout, 4–5x in week, including 1 strength + 1 walk |
| Steps | 8,500/day |
| Eat | Three protein-anchored meals; one mindful indulgence |
| Hydrate | 3L |
| Sleep | 7 hr average |
| Check-in | Daily |

Coach actions:
- Day 15: introduce "what does week 4 look like for you?" (post-cohort planning)
- Day 18: "tell us your win" — collect testimonial seeds
- Day 21: final progress photo + weight + waist + final reflection

## Phase 7 — Wrap + Champion (Days 22–25)

- Day 22: All users submit Champion entry (see `03_Champion_Program/Submission_Template.md`)
- Day 23: Judging
- Day 24: Top 3 announced live on Instagram + email + WhatsApp group
- Day 25: Cohort completion certificate + offer to enrol in next cohort or upgrade to monthly plan

## Daily WhatsApp rhythm

| Time | Message |
|---|---|
| 7:00 AM | "Good morning" + today's habit focus + workout link |
| 1:00 PM | Lunch nudge: photo your plate, score it 1-5 |
| 7:00 PM | Evening check: water, steps, dinner reminder |
| 9:30 PM | Wind-down nudge: tomorrow prep + sleep cue |

Users reply once per day minimum. Coach reviews replies in 2-hour batches (10 AM, 3 PM, 9 PM).

---

## Cohort group rules (WhatsApp community)

1. No selling, no spam, no DMs to other members
2. No body comparison or "you should..." advice — only encouragement
3. Coach is the only one who gives nutrition or workout direction
4. Medical questions get redirected to "please consult your doctor"
5. Wins, struggles, food photos, walk photos all welcome

---

## Success criteria for the cohort

- Completion rate ≥ 60% (defined: checked in ≥ 15 of 21 days)
- NPS ≥ 40
- ≥ 5 named transformations with full before/after
- ≥ 1 piece of organic UGC per 5 users
- ≤ 5% refund rate
