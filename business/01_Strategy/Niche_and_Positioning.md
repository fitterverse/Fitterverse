# Niche and Positioning

## The customer

**Persona name:** "Restart Rahul" / "Restart Riya"

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| Age | 25–40 |
| City | Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Pune, Delhi NCR, Mumbai (tier-1 metros first) |
| Income | ₹8L–₹35L per year |
| Job | Desk-bound — IT, consulting, finance, marketing, ops |
| Work hours | 9–11 hours, often with 1+ hour commute |
| Family | Mostly unmarried or newly married, may live with parents |
| Fitness past | Has tried at least 2 things — gym, app, diet, trainer — and quit each within 6–10 weeks |
| Current state | 5–15 kg over their target, low energy, stress eating, weekend binge, late dinners, irregular sleep |
| Devices | Smartphone-first, WhatsApp daily, Instagram daily, may own a smartwatch |
| Willingness to pay | ₹500–₹3,000/month if trust is built; ₹999 for a clear 21-day program is an easy yes |

## The pain (in their words)

- "I know what to do. I just can't stick to it."
- "Apps make me feel guilty when I miss a day, then I uninstall."
- "I can't track Indian food. Calorie counters don't get my dal-rice."
- "Gym at 7am sounded great until my 11pm calls started."
- "I'll start Monday." (said every Monday)

## The promise

> Build your first consistent fitness routine in 21 days — without crash diets, gym obsession, or an app you'll uninstall by week two.

## The wedge (why this niche, why now)

1. **Lowest friction price point** — ₹999 is below the "I need to think about it" threshold for this income bracket
2. **Highest density of pain** — busy professionals are the largest pool of fitness restart-quitters in India
3. **WhatsApp-native behavior** — this cohort opens WhatsApp 50+ times a day; we meet them where they are
4. **Cohort identity beats app loyalty** — saying "I'm doing the Fitterverse Reset" is socially shareable in a way "I use a fitness app" isn't

## The defensible position

> "The Indian-context cohort accountability system for busy professionals."

Three things compound over time and become moats:
1. **Indian food behavior data** — what people actually eat, miss, and binge on
2. **Cohort completion playbook** — the SOPs and nudges that drive 60%+ completion
3. **Champion case studies** — every cohort produces 3 named winners, building a transformation library no competitor can copy
